Remember when Joey Tribianni gave Rachel Green lilies (her favorite flower, of course!) on their date and how her heart melted and it was just an “Aww…” moment on screen? You can definitely make that same heartwarming moment in real life with lilies, the flowers of royalty! While every color holds a different specific meaning, lilies in general represent royalty, purity, passion, and rebirth. They’re mostly seen during weddings and given during birthdays, mothers’ day, valentine’s day, and even anniversaries. Lilies have the power to melt hearts, and even make smiles that are as pure as your intentions.

Lilies can be used in a variety of arrangements, from bouquets to centerpieces or simply just because. Offering you freshness and sweetness, our lilies are among our most popular bouquets at Flower Chimp Philippines. We have the perfect bouquet to spruce up your comfort zone that benefits you in many ways. Not only does it help you with your beauty sleep, it also purifies the air to give you that extra freshness! Lillies are a lovely and thoughtful gift - whether to celebrate a newborn baby, a declaration of love, or simply to express your feelings of admiration for someone, lilies are a beautiful and eye-catching centrepiece. Our assortment of lilies, including varieties such as "Carina" and "Lily Love," each come with an elegant vase that is adaptable to any home or office.

With the right kind of care, lilies may maintain their stunning appearance for up to two weeks. It is preferable to remove the leaves that are situated close to the stem's base in order to prevent lilies from becoming wilted. At the very least, you should switch out the water in the vase once every few days in order to maintain its quality!
